Socialites

Just wanna ride and make new friends? No problem.

SF Bike Party

  • Every first Friday starting at 8pm -- Today!
  • Route is announced the day of
  • Themed flash mob of bikes and loud music touring the city

Midnight Mystery Ride

  • Every third Saturday
  • Meeting location is announced the day of the ride.
  • Show up at midnight and follow the leader.

Butterlap

Sports Basement Riders Club

  • Every Sunday starting at 8:45 am

Tour de Tandem

  • Sunday, May 4th starting at 11 am
  • Easy-paced ride from the Panhandle to Ocean Beach and back.

  • Sunday, May 4 @ 1pm

Sewer Ride

  • Sunday, May 10 @ 12pm
  • Explore the squishy boundary between humans and H2O, with emphasis on the massive sewer mechanics below the city.

  • Friday, May 23 @ 4pm
  • Surprise route to explore the culture, art and history of SF.
